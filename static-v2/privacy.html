Who we are

Elevatek is a trade name of Elevatek Consulting Inc., a company incorporated in Québec, Canada. For anything privacy-related, write to info@elevatek.ca. Our designated privacy officer can be reached at the same address.

What we collect

When you use the contact form, we receive what you typed: your name, work email, company, role, stack details, and your message. When you subscribe to the newsletter, we keep your email address. Our hosting provider keeps standard server logs.

Why we collect it

To reply to you, to scope and deliver consulting work you ask us about, and to send the newsletter you signed up for. We don't sell or rent your information, and we don't use it for advertising.

How long we keep it

Contact submissions are kept as long as needed to handle your request and any engagement that follows. Newsletter emails are kept until you unsubscribe, which you can do from any issue.

Your rights

Under PIPEDA and Québec's Law 25, you can ask what personal information we hold about you, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it. Email info@elevatek.ca and we'll handle it.
