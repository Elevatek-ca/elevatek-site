The problem

Lead to cash is one process. Your stack treats it like eight.

Somewhere between quote and invoice, sales hands the deal to finance and the CRM hands it to the ERP. That seam is where the numbers start drifting. Most of what gets called a reporting problem is a handoff problem.

FAQ

Questions people actually ask.

Do you replace our ops team or work alongside it?

Alongside, almost always. Fractional means we plug into your existing team, take a slice of the backlog, and hand back working systems plus the documentation to run them. If you have no ops team yet, we can be it for a while, then help you hire.

Our Salesforce project is already off the rails. Can you step in mid-flight?

Yes. Rescue work usually starts with a short audit: what was promised, what was built, what's salvageable. You get that picture before deciding whether we take over the build.

Salesforce or HubSpot, which one do you push?

Neither by default. We work in both ecosystems and recommend based on your team, your budget, and what you already own. Sometimes the honest answer is "keep what you have and fix the plumbing."

What does fractional actually mean in hours?

Anywhere from a few hours a week to a few days. We size it together during the free session, and you can scale it up or down between sprints.

Do you work in French?

Oui. We're Canadian and run projects in French, English, or both, including documentation and training.

Where do you work from?

Canada. We work remotely with clients across the Americas and Europe, in English or French.

What happens when the project ends?

You keep everything: documentation, decision logs, and a trained team. Some clients move to a light retainer for upkeep. Some don't need us anymore. Both outcomes count as success on our end.
